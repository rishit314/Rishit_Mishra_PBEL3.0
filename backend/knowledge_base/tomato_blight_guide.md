# Tomato Early Blight Extension Guide
## Symptoms
Early blight (Alternaria solani) causes dark, concentric rings (target-spot pattern) on older leaves.

## Cultural Controls
- Rotate crops every 2-3 years.
- Prune lower leaves to increase airflow and prevent soil splash.
- Use drip irrigation instead of overhead watering.

## Chemical & Organic Controls
- **Organic:** Apply Copper Fungicide or Bacillus subtilis every 7-10 days.
- **Chemical:** Apply Chlorothalonil (FRAC M05) or Mancozeb at first sign of symptoms. Do not apply within 7 days of harvest.
